About Zairza Software Wing

"Man is a slow, sloppy, and brilliant thinker. Computers are fast, accurate and stupid. When they get together they are invincible."

John Pfeiffer

Computers have been a prominent reason of technological advancement to the present day. Be it electronics, medicine, pure sciences or any such field, computers have established themselves necessary everywhere.

Zairza software wing of the club blends the brilliance of engineering minds with the pace and memory of computers. Interfacing the computer hardware with cool, new and effective ideas is the basic target of this club.

This wing focuses on creating and developing new software with research on variety of assembly and high level languages. It also does a considerable amount of efforts in web development and website designing. Interactive software are developed by thorough research and testing.

The students form a hierarchical set up with the seniors and alumni forming the chief advisors or mentors and juniors working with the valuable advices from their mentors. Extensive research and software development is carried out by all the students in a collaborative manner.

Research fields include coding in high level languages as Python, Ruby, and designing web pages with variety of interesting tools, android app development and a lot more.
