About Zairza Robotics Wing

"Any sufficiently advanced technology is Indistinguishable from magic."

Arthur C. Clarke

Well developed technology brings about a magical transformation to the lives of mankind, making them more comfortable and pleasurable to live in. It can be credited with the drastic changes in human thought process which have inspired a million new ideas and devices that changed the world.

Zairza robotics society is a wing of the Zairza club which is instrumental in developing as well implementing modern day technology. It helps integrate engineering innovation into a variety of machines and technical devices. Research and creation of new products form the basic targets of this wing.

This wing stresses on development of robots, modern electronic devices, mechanical instruments and a whole lot of other engineering fields. It consists of a brilliant group of engineering enthusiasts who have exceptional interest in innovating and inventing technical products.

The students form a hierarchical set up with the seniors and alumni forming the chief advisors or mentors and juniors working with the valuable advices from their mentors. Extensive research and product development is carried out by all the students in a collaborative manner.

Research fields include Robotics, Microcontrollers and their programming, Sixth Sense, Control and Automation and a lot more...
