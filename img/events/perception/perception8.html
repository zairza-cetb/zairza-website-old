      “Karobaar” is a cross functional business management game held during Xtasy – The annual fest of CET,BBSR.

      This peep into the world of management coats the rigor and vitality of a career in management with a strong dose of fun.

      The object...ive of this game is to give them a glimpse of MBA education and also to help them in making the right decision in future.

      Karobaar consists of following modules:- Marketing, Finance, Operations, HR.

      The Karobaar challenge is to simplify MBA jargon so that it is understandable by under-graduate students. We have games in Finance, Marketing, Operations, HR but everything is fine tuned to suit the participants who don’t know about these much.

      Each team has to consist of 2 people.Teams need to register themselves in the following link-

      https://docs.google.com/forms/d/16_I40ivbXW2Nrs32ZluxQeqf74l1rqSNvu_20_TYvxc/viewform

      The registered teams have to appear for the preliminary round which will be held offline on FEBRUARY 19TH and consist of aptitude, reasoning and GK questions.. The 12 finalist teams are selected based on their performance in the preliminary round.

      Here it comes folks... details of the event 'De Papel Presentation' start juggling through all the brilliant ideas that come into your mind and give it your best shot.

      Here are the details:

      Theme : Technology for improvement of society
      ...
      Topics :
      The participants must submit entries that must address one or more of the following issues
      1. Technology for empowering rural population
      2. Innovations for disaster management
      3. Technologies for the welfare of the society

      Rules:

      1. Teams of at most 2 participants are allowed
      2.The teams must send in an abstract for the prelims .
      3. The abstract should be mailed to xtasy2k14@gmail.com
      4. The abstract has to reach us before 19th February,2014
      5. The abstracts shall be judges by our panel of judges and the best shall be selected to appear for the finals

      6. The teams must send in an abstract using the following format-

      i. Title

      ii. Abstract

      a. Objectives
      b. Beneficiaries (For whom)
      c. Value of results (Use)

      7. In the final round , the teams shall have to make a presentation on the topic at CET during Xtasy'14 and showcase their idea infront of our panel of judges
      8. The decision of the judges shall be final and binding
